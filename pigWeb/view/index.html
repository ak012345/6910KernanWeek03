Play Pig!


A game of chance where you must balance being bold vs. greedy

A little insight into the game:

Pig is a class of game often referred to as a jeopardy game; that is, you will have to make decisions that could cost you everything or might win you big. It requires a balance between getting the most points without losing ground, and without falling hopelessly behind your competitors. This is a great game to pass the time and, because it only requires only one set of Dice, making it a ton of fun for its highly portable and fun nature. There are several variations, so please head over to the "How to Play" section to learn more
