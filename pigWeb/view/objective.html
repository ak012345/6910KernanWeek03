Primary Objective of PIG

The objective of this game is simple: Be the first player to attain the desired score.

To achieve the desired score, players take turns rolling one or more dice, adding up the numbers on their faces.
Each Player's turn consists of making a choice; that is, roll again to try for a higher score or pass the die and take the points already achieved.

You might think this sounds simple enough, and it is, but there are a few rules that force you to take turns, test your daring, and change up strategies. To learn how to play, please take a look at the instructions available in the How to Play section above.
