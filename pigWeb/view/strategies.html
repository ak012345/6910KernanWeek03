Strategies often seen within the game:

Early Lead

The "Early Lead" strategy is the "hail mary" power play where a player will take large risks early on to pull out a solid lead amongst the other opponents. Generally considered very high risk, but can push other players who are risk-averse to make mistakes.

Pickup The Smalls

The "Pickup the Smalls" strategy is the safest and most conservative strategy where a person only attempts to pickup the minimum amount of points to stay competitive.

Alternate Risk

This strategy is a systematic alteration between low risk/conservative plays where a person gets a sufficient number of points to stay relevant, on one turn, but increases risk and extends themselves on the next turn to attempt to pull away from the other competitors.
The Set number of throws strategy is quite simple. A person decides on a number they are comfortable throwing per turn and stops there. (provided they don't get the forbidden turn-ending number, of course)
